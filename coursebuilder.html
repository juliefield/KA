Seamless course delivery at your fingertips

Flexible, intuitive and modern, our LMS can reinvent the way you develop talent and build leaders within your organization.

Simple, Yet Flexible Course Authoring


You shouldn’t have to compromise ease of use for more creative control, and with our Course Builder you don’t have to. Create immersive, multimedia-rich courses from an existing template in minutes. Or, break out your inner Picasso and start with a blank canvas. Either way, you’ll find that our intuitive user interface and robust authoring controls make it easy to create courses with the “wow” factor.

Cloud-based Collaboration

Accelerate content creation cycles and create synergy between multiple course developers with a cloud-based, cross-platform authoring environment. With our scalable course development tool, subject matter experts across different departments can quickly and effortlessly build, review and revise course content to ensure it stays consistent and relevant as your organization grows.

Rapid Course Publishing


Reach your audience from any location or device with fast, one-click publishing. With the tap of a button you can publish SCORM 1.2- and Tin Can API 1.0-compliant content to your Learning Management System in just seconds. And since all courses created using Course Builder are HTML5-, JavaScript and iPad-ready you can rest assured that they will load quickly on any device
