It’s time to move beyond the traditional LMS.

Make training natural instead of something that feels forced by HR with our flexible, intuitive and modern training platform. Created with the 21st century learner in mind.

Deliver and manage content with ease


Empower your workforce to succeed with an online training solution that incorporates improved accessibility, transparency and meaningful, relevant content. With our centralized learning platform you can deliver virtual learning, webinars, instructor-led training, assessments, certifications and compliance content on virtually any mobile device.

Build custom learning paths

Improve individual ownership over skill development and inspire your workforce to learn more by assigning relevant, job-specific training curriculum. With our LMS, you can build and prescribe targeted learning paths to individuals or groups that address identified gaps in skills and desired performance.

Exceed the latest regulatory requirements


Improve workforce compliance and enhance the prestige of certificates earned through your LMS by delivering authenticated certificates that can be added to a user’s LinkedIn profile. All certificates earned through our LMS come with a unique identification number and an expiration date to avoid duplication.

Collect and measure meaningful data

Our dynamic, intuitive reporting dashboard will provide you with the easy data access you need to get visibility into the effectiveness of your training program Measure the business impact of your LMS with real-time user reports, or export user data to excel to build custom reports that help support high-level business planning.

Get the royal treatment

Our LMS is not an island. Simply building a training platform with the expectation that our users will be able to optimize its use once they’ve made the investment is not in our DNA. Wiith your own dedicated customer success representative, unlimited phone support and 24-hour email support, you can be sure your training program will shine. We guarantee a legendary experience.

More Features

Single-Sign-On (SSO)

Eliminate the need to remember multiple username and password combinations with an LMS that effortlessly integrates with your SSO solution.

Localization-ready*

Engage a global audience with a multilingual user interface that can be translated into any language that you need.

Social Sharing

Make it easy for your audience to communicate beyond the lines of the virtual classroom with built-in social buttons that link course content to LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter and Google +.

Content Compatibility

Mobile Learning

E-commerce
